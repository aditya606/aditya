About Us

We care is a network of hospital in india.Founded in 2019 as a autonomas institution to serve as a nucleus for nurtuing excellence in all aspect of health care.

Twenty-five clinical department including four super specialty centers manages practically all types of disease conditions with support from pre-and Para-clinical departments However, burn cases,and patients suffering from infection diseases are not entertained in the we care Hospital. we care also a 60-beded hospital in the Comprehensive Rural Health Centre at Panchkula in Haryana and provides health cover to about 2.5 lakh population through the centers for community Medicine.
